The SRM Cube Club is a community for speed cubers and puzzle enthusiasts around the campus. The club was founded by Abhijeet Ghodgaonkar and Shivam Sharma in 2016. We have achieved several feats in these past few years, also having had our name in the India Book of Records for making the largest mosaic with around 6000 Rubik's Cubes, put on display during Milan'17. We have also conducted the SRM Cube Open for three years in a row, attracting speed cubers from all over the nation and also from abroad. For Milan '19, we had several light events which attracted both cubers and non-cubers, primarily focusing on the latter to get more people to be a part of the community. Being a club consisting of various podium achievers and multiple Guinness World Record holders, the primary objective of the SRM Cube Club is to improve and expand the Cubing community. A Rubik's cube is a symbol of intellect and is something which commonly invokes intimidation, something that we strive to eradicate. In our endeavor to make speed cubing a more popular sport than it is now, we continue to work towards conducting more workshops, seminars, quality competitions and break more records.
