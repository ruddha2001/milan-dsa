painting competition

Unleash your skills in painting and bring out the Picasso and Botticelli in you all. Show your creativity with brush strokes and Water/Acrylic colors.

Judging Criteria

Judging shall be based on the depiction of the theme, aesthetic appeal, painting skills and creativity shown in the painting. 

Rules

1. This is an individual event. Participants should at least report to the venue 20 minutes prior to the commencement of Competition.
2. The event will be conducted in one round.
3. Participants are needed to make a painting based on a theme given to them on the spot. The time limit is 2 hours.
4. All logistics shall be provided on the spot. No additional logistics apart from those provided in the beginning can be used.
5. Use of any outside aids and internet is strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification. Painting should be made only on the paper provided in the venue.
6. All necessary stationeries can be carried by the participants. Only basic stationeries will be available in the venue which will include pencils, erasers, scales and basic colors.

road graffiti/painting

The mega art event where you can unleash not only your skills in art, but showcase your passion and dedication towards it. Making Art bigger and better, for whom conventional is not the way of life. Painting on papers and canvases? Huh, too conventional! Hold the road as your ultimate canvas.

Judging Criteria

Judging shall be based on the theme given, creativity, painting skills, completion of the painting and neatness.

Rules

1. This is a.group event. A maximum of 3 participants are allowed.
2. Participants should at least report to the venue 20 minutes prior to the commencement of Competition.
3. The event will be conducted in two round. First round will be of 20 minutes, the participant has to sketch the design on paper that they will be wanting to paint on the road based on the theme provided on the spot.
4. Round 2 will be the final round where the participants will paint or make Graffiti on the road. The time limit for round two is 3 hours.
5. There will be elimination in round one and only the best top designs will go on to the next round.
6. All logistics shall be provided on the spot. No additional logistics apart from those provided in the beginning can be used.
7. Use of any outside aids and internet is strictly prohibited and may lead to disqualification.
8. Colors, papers and basic stationeries will be provided on the venue. The participants can carry their own colors if required.
