volley ball

general rules:

- FIVB rules will be followed throughout the tournament.

- A minimum of 6 players and a maximum of 12 players in a team.

- 3-set matches to 25 points . Each set must be won by two points. The winner is the first team to     win 2 sets.

- For semi-finals and finals 5-set matches to 25 points . Each set must be won by two points .The     winner is the first team to win three sets.

- Teams must report 1 hour prior to the court and no extra time will be given for warm up, if late.

- Maximum delay in reporting will be 15 minutes, else walkover will be given.

- Teams must bring their own adequate volleyballs for practice. No balls will be provided from the     institute for practice.

- The volleyball team is not accountable or responsible in any manner for the teams conflicting     schedules. 

NOTE:
The prepared Volleyball matches schedule will not be reformed according to our convenience.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:

RULES & SCHEDULE ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE IN THE SPIRIT OF THE GAME .THE FINAL DECISION IN ALL MATTERS RESTS WITH THE MILAN COLOSSEUM TEAM. THE MANAGEMENT ALSO RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CANCEL AN EVENT AT ANY POINT OF THE FEST.
