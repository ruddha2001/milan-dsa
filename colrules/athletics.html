GENERAL RULES:

-In a standard track of eight lanes, distribution of lanes and qualifying to subsequence round will be done as per standard rules for 100m, 200m, 400m run.

-An athlete can participate in a maximum of two individual events (any two), excluding relays .

-In an individual event , there can be maximum 4 athletes for a college and 1 relay team for a college.

-The athletes need to confirm their events during verification. Same to be done for relay as a team.

-If in any event the number of participating athletes is less than 7, then bronze medal i.e. 3rd position will not be awarded for that event. If the participating athletes are less than 4, then only gold medal will be awarded .

-Decisions of the referees would be final and no objections would be entertained in the result.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:

RULES & SCHEDULE ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE IN THE SPIRIT OF THE GAME .THE FINAL DECISION IN ALL MATTERS RESTS WITH THE MILAN COLOSSEUM TEAM. THE MANAGEMENT ALSO RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CANCEL AN EVENT AT ANY POINT OF THE FEST.
